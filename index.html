Creating an Affirming Environment for LGBTQ+ Community

EASY WAYS TO BE INCLUSIVE



Include LGBTQ

We should include LGBTQ+ themed materials in classrooms and offices without drawing special attention to them. This way, we will help to create an atmosphere where we normalise all sexualities and help LGBTQ people not feel excluded for their gender or sexual identities.



Use Inclusive Language

This includes talking in a way that does not specify a gender, sex, or sexual orientation unless it is pertinent to the comment. This implies the use of gender neutral terms like “partner” or “significant other” instead of specifying “husband" or "wife".


Don't form Opinions

Remember that you don’t know anyone’s sexual orientation or gender identity unless they tell you. So never assume anybody's sexuality based on your personal opinions. The LGBTQ+ Community has as much diversity as there is among those who identify as heterosexual.
